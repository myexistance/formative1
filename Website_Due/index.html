  • Welcome to Yoobee School of Design, and in particular the Level 6 Diploma in Web and UX Design leading to the qualification New Zealand Diploma in Web Design and Production. Yoobee School of Design maintains regular links with industry to ensure the course evolves along with industry standards and local employment demands. We also value student feedback and you will get regular opportunities to express your opinions and make recommendations to us. We wish you all the best over the coming year. You'll get out whatever you put in, so work hard and enjoy!

  • WELLINGTON CAMPUS Expand the frontiers of your digital world at our Wellington Campus. This dynamic hub was a finalist in the Creative category for the 2014 Wellington Gold Awards. It features 9 computer labs, three theory rooms, a large A/V Studio featuring a green screen and sound booth with the latest equipment, and a flatbed and pull down screen for photography. Film and Animation are the superstars here and Wellywood’s growing film and animation industry means there are plenty of opportunities to immerse yourself in this area. Wellington is also a thriving creative capital with strong demand for design and web programmes producing high quality designers and thinkers.
